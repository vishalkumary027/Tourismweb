BACKPurandar Fort

The fort of Purandar is well known as it is the birthplace of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, the first son of Great Maratha ruler Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj was even the successor of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Pune District, Maharashtra, India. Historical records mention that Purandar belongs to the 11th century Yadava Dynasty. The Persians defeated the Yadavas and conquered the area and, in 1350, they built the Purandar Fort. It was ruled by the government during the reign of the Ahmednagar and Bijapur Kings.
